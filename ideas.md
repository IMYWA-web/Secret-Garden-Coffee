## Selected Design: **Analog Warmth — Botanical Edition** (Option 3)

Chosen for its authentic, handcrafted personality that matches Secret Garden Coffee's identity as a lush, cozy garden retreat. The vintage botanical poster aesthetic, editorial layout, and warm forest-green palette will make the site feel distinctive and memorable — evoking the "bridgerton vibe" and "rabbits in the garden" atmosphere described in reviews.
